Maserati Service App

Maserati Service App

The Maserati Service App is an application for young Maserati owners (ages 25-40) which allows them to experience the personalized luxary post-sales service in order to increase brand loyalty and result in future return purchases and sustainable business growth.

Slides

My Role

Maserati Service was a project done in four weeks. My team and I went to the Maserati Dealership to get information about their problems. Throughout the project, I was incharge of the ideation process and interactions of the interface, along with some motion graphics. I also filmed and edited the video that introduces our interface with Aftereffects.

Business Problem

The core problem within Maserati is the lack of brand loyalty among younger generation customers due to lack of post-sale service. This results in lack of return and potential purchases.

Through research and asking questions from Maserati Dealerships, their sales for Maserati was fine, but the main problem was that customers tend to switch into another brand due to lack of service after the purchase.

Opening

The Service

The idea of Maserati Service is to have everything about their Maserati (Car Maintenance, Appointments, Scheduling) easily taken care of for the customer. Customers will be notified when their car is in need of service with actionable information. Maserati Service also allow customers to schedule and remind them of appointment along with pickup and drop off service for the vehicle so that it is not mandatory to visit the maintenance shop. Payment is also done in the application with the service history saved.

Journey Framework

We made a journey framework for Maserati Service to speculate how customers will use and what they will think when they use the application.
