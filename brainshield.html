BrainShield

BrainShield

BrainShield is the first of it's kind functional decal made of micro-engineered layers that diverts angled impacts and improve helmet performance. Our team made a video for BrainShield in order to promote this product to the market.

My Role

For this project, my team and I went to interview all the interviewees which includes setting up the cameras, mics, and lighting. Post interview, I edited the video in Premiere Pro and made sure it transitions smoothly. I also took pictures of helmet stills and other B-Rolls for the video.

Interviews and B-Rolls

Throughout this project, we have interviewed with many different people, including football coaches, professors, athletes, and sports enthusiasts

We also went to many different places to film B-Rolls including hiking trails, parks, empty roads. One of the most exciting things we did was filming a motorcyle rider. Finding a location and chasing the motorcycle while keeping the camera stable was very difficult, but also rewarding.
